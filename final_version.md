One of the most pressing challenges in Healthcare Technology today is clinical documentation burden. Physicians and nurses spend a disproportionate amount of their time entering notes, coding diagnoses, and completing administrative records — often at the expense of direct patient care. Studies have shown that clinicians can spend more than half of their working hours on documentation rather than with patients, contributing to widespread burnout and reduced quality of care.

Generative AI holds significant promise for alleviating this burden:

- **Automated clinical note generation:** By leveraging large language models, we can capture doctor-patient dialogues in real-time. This allows for the automatic generation of structured notes (like SOAP notes), effectively "giving back" hours of the day to clinicians so they can focus on what matters: the person sitting across from them.

- **Intelligent coding and billing assistance:** Generative AI can analyze clinical narratives and suggest accurate ICD and CPT billing codes, minimizing errors and reducing the time staff spend on administrative coding tasks.
- **Context-aware documentation summarization:** AI can synthesize information across a patient's longitudinal health records — lab results, prior visit notes, imaging reports — into concise, relevant summaries that help clinicians quickly understand a patient's history without manually reviewing extensive records.
